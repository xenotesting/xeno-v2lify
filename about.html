About Xeno

Xeno began before Summer 2024, originally known as FixMyDoge using Doge Unblocker. In October 2024, the idea evolved into a full hub — not just for bypassing filters, but for sharing useful tools, games, emulators, and fun features.

The UI started violet, shifted to a Discord-like blurple in November (peak Xeno), and then moved to pink during Quarter 4 as Doge Unblocker was removed and replaced by Utopia. With time, Seraph (a massive open-source game collection) was added. Utopia was eventually removed due to low usage.

Now in Summer 2025, Xeno is more streamlined and focused on Games, Emulators, and Built-in Features. You’re on the official Netlify version, with more surprises on the way.
