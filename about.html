About Xeno v2.0

Xeno started before the summer of 2024 as a random idea. Editing officially began in October 2024 after the project was inspired out of nowhere.

Before it was Xeno, it was called FixMyDoge. The site used Doge Unblocker and was focused on bypassing filters. Eventually, Xeno became more than that — a hub for games, tools, and other fun features.

The UI started violet and changed to a blurple Discord-like color in November 2024. That was peak Xeno, where most links stayed unblocked for weeks.

When Doge’s servers went down permanently, Utopia took over. Utopia Links and Seraph Links were introduced during Quarter 4 — Seraph being an open-source game library with 200+ games.

Now in summer 2025, Utopia has been phased out due to low usage. Xeno’s focus has shifted to Games, Emulators, and Built-in Features.

You might see some new features added soon — possibly even some JavaScript tools — but Xeno's spirit remains: fun, flexible, and refreshingly random.
