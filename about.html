About Xeno v2.0

Xeno started before the summer of 2024 as a random idea. Editing officially began in October 2024 after the project was inspired out of nowhere.

Before it was Xeno, it was called FixMyDoge. The site used Doge Unblocker and was focused on bypassing the school's networks.. Eventually, Xeno became more than that — a hub for game links, unblocker links, and other fun links.

The UI started a nice violet/purple color and changed to a blurple Discord-like color in January 2025. That was peak Xeno, where most links stayed unblocked for weeks, sometimes a month or two if kinda lucky.

When Doge’s servers went down permanently, Utopia replaced our beloved Doge Unblocker. Utopia Links and Seraph Links were introduced during Quarter 4 — Seraph being an open-source game library with 200+ games.

Now in Summer 2025, Utopia has unfortunately left Xeno due to low usage. Xeno’s focus has shifted to games, emulators, and other built-in features.

You might see some new features added soon — possibly even some JavaScript tools — but Xeno's spirit remains: very stupid and flexible.
